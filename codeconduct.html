REGISTRATION FORM

INTERN CODE OF CONDUCT

I, ..................... the undersigned intern, do hereby undertake to abide by the following Code of Conduct. Any breach will result in disciplinary action against me, which may lead to expulsion without any further notice.

1. Interns are permitted to eat and drink only in designated areas of the workplace and at the designated times as stipulated by the agreed-upon terms of the employer.

2. Smoking will not be allowed at the workplace, or as stipulated by the agreed-upon terms of the employer.

3. Students shall not consume alcohol at the workplace or any other narcotic substances or have them in their possession.

4. Any damage to the workplace property through willful acts or negligence will not be allowed.

5. Students shall not engage in any act of violence, threaten violence, or carry weapons to the workplace environment.

6. Studentsâ€™ clothes and general appearance should in every respect reflect an awareness of the workplace environment and should be conducive to that work environment.

7. Students are not permitted to bring friends or visitors to the workplace environment without the prior consent of the workplace supervisor.

8. Students are responsible for all resources under their control.

9. Students shall comply with rules and regulations published by GTL as well as with the establishment of employment from time to time.

10. Students will perform their duties with skill, care and diligence according to the objectives, standards and expected outcomes of GTL as well as with the establishment of employment.

11. Students will acquaint themselves with all information that pertains to their duties and responsibilities.

12. Students will seek further guidance or training as required.

13. Students will observe all Acts, Regulations, instructions and lawful directions relating to their official duties.

14. Students will behave at all times in a manner that maintains or enhances the reputation of GTL and the establishment of employment and adhere to all policies and procedures of GTL and the establishment of employment.
